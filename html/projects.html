  • Evolution

    JavaFX program in which a flock of smart birds teach themselves to play a recreation of the notorious mobile game Flappy Bird. Uses a 3-layer neural network to make momentary decision of whether or not each bird should jump. Most successful birds' neural nets are mutated and propogated to the next generation, to simulate "Evolution".
