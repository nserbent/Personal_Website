  • Evolution

    Java program in which a flock of smart birds teach themselves to play a recreation of the notorious mobile game Flappy Bird. Uses a 3-layer neural network to make momentary decision of whether or not each bird should jump. Most successful birds' neural nets are mutated and propogated to the next generation, to simulate "Evolution".

  • IndyGame
    Winner of annual CS15 'Cartoon' competition @Brown

    This flash game is entitled "The_Easy_Way", and is my homage to my favorite fantastic work of all time: LOTR. Here, I am making my own contribution to the well-trodden fan theoretical subject of easier ways they may have destroyed the One Ring. It depicts a sword controlled by the player that can interact with a falling ring. The goal of the game s to successfully knock the ring into the mouth of the volcano, Mt. Doom. Throughout the cartoon, the eye of Sauron in the tower on screen will talk to the player, with unique dialouge dependent on some in game events.
